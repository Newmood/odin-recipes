Dalgona Coffee

Description

Dalgona coffee is delightful drink originated in South Korea. It is easy to make and has many customizations, such as adding chocolate, honey and spices. Can be served in both warm or cold milk, on top of which sits whipped coffee mixture which also gives it a beautiful look!

Let us look at the preparation of Dalgona coffee below, with addition of honey and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon cocoa powder (or chocolate syrup)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon honey (adjust to taste)
  • Milk (¾ cup)
  • Optional: Ice cubes (to serve cold)
  • Optional: Additional cocoa/chocolate to garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, add instant coffee, sugar,hot water, cocoa powder and honey.
  2. Using a whisk/hand mixer, whip the mixture until it becomes thick and frothy.
  3. In a mug, pour the warm milk or put in ice cubes then add milk if you prefer cold.
  4. Spoon the whipped mixture of coffee on top of the milk.
  5. Dust some cocoa powder on top or add chocolate syrup to garnish.
  6. Serve and enjoy !!
