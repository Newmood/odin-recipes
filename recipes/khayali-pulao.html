Khayali Pulao

Description

Khayali Pulao is an idiomatic expression that nearly translates to "castles in the air", commonly used among Hindi and Urdu language speakers. The word "khayal" means thought/imagination and Pulao is a real dish made of rice and vegetables often with egg or even meat.

Here we look at one approach towards making khayali pulao, try out with your own variations!

Ingredients

  • Some imaginative superpowers
  • Bunch of old memories
  • The will of thinking
  • One daydreaming session
  • Creativity (to garnish)

Instructions

  1. Fire up your daydreaming session.
  2. Take the old memory into consideration, clearly remember what you did in that memory.
  3. Use the will of thinking to think what you could have done better than what you did back then.
  4. Use your imaginative superpowers to construct an ideal world based on how your present would have changed based on the things you could have done better.
  5. Use your creativity, to give yourself a completely new style than what can happen in reality.
  6. In this ideal world, enjoy!
